How Deposits Work

Depositing funds into your account is a simple and secure process that allows you to start staking and earning rewards. Below is an explanation of how the deposit system works on our platform:

1. Supported Cryptocurrencies

Our platform supports multiple cryptocurrencies for deposits, including:

  • Bitcoin (BTC)
  • Ethereum (ETH)
  • Dogecoin (DOGE)
  • Litecoin (LTC)
  • Bitcoin Cash (BCH)
  • Dash (DASH)
  • DigiByte (DGB)
  • Tron (TRX)
  • Tether TRC20 (USDT)
  • Feyorra (FEY)
  • Zcash (ZEC)
  • Binance Coin (BNB)
  • Solana (SOL)
  • Ripple (XRP)
  • Polygon (MATIC)
  • Cardano (ADA)
  • Toncoin (TON)
  • Stellar (XLM)
  • USD Coin (USDC)
  • Monero (XMR)
  • Taraxa (TARA)

2. How to Deposit

  • Select Cryptocurrency: First, you will select the cryptocurrency that you want to use for your deposit.
  • Enter Amount: You will then enter the amount you wish to deposit. Please ensure you meet the minimum deposit requirements for the selected cryptocurrency.
  • Transaction Processing: After submitting your deposit request, you will be redirected to FaucetPay to complete the transaction with your selected cryptocurrency.

  • Bitcoin Cash (BCH): 0.00000001 BCH

  • Taraxa (TARA): 0.00000001 TARA

4. Deposit Fees

There is a small deposit fee for each transaction to cover the processing cost. The fee will be automatically deducted from the total amount of your deposit.

5. Transaction Confirmation

After completing your deposit transaction, it may take some time for the funds to be confirmed on the blockchain. Once the transaction is confirmed, the deposited amount will be reflected in your account balance, and you'll be able to use it for staking.

6. Safety and Security

All deposit transactions are processed securely through FaucetPay, and the funds will only be credited to your account after the transaction has been successfully confirmed on the blockchain. You can track the status of your deposit from your History.
