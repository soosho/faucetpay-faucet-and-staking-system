How the Level System Works

At our platform, the level system allows you to earn experience points (EXP) and level up through your daily activities, such as claiming rewards and playing games. As you progress to higher levels, you unlock bonuses that enhance your daily earnings. Here’s a breakdown of how it works:

1. How to Earn EXP

  • Daily Claims: Earn 1 EXP for each daily claim you make. You can claim up to 50 times a day.
  • Dice Rolls: Each dice roll you perform also grants 1 EXP. Play games and level up faster!

2. Level-Up Bonuses

As you reach higher levels, you receive a percentage bonus that is added to your daily claim rewards. Here’s how the bonus system works:

  • Bonus Percentage: Each time you level up, your bonus percentage increases, providing additional rewards with every claim you make.
  • Example: If your base daily claim reward is $0.0005, and you have a level bonus of 10%, you will receive an extra 10% on top of the base amount for each claim.

3. Reward Calculation Example

Let’s see how your daily claim reward is calculated when you have a level bonus:

  • Base Claim Amount: $0.0005 per claim
  • Level Bonus: 10%
  • Bonus Amount: 10% of $0.0005 = $0.00005
  • Total Claim Amount: $0.0005 + $0.00005 = $0.00055 per claim
  • If you make all 50 daily claims, your total earnings would be 50 x $0.00055 = $0.0275 per day.

4. Level Progression

To level up, you need to accumulate the required EXP for each level. Here’s how your progression looks from level 1 to 100:

  • Level 1: Requires 1 EXP
  • Level 2: Requires 10 EXP
  • Level 3: Requires 21 EXP
  • Level 4: Requires 34 EXP
  • Level 5: Requires 50 EXP
  • Level 6: Requires 69 EXP
  • Level 7: Requires 91 EXP
  • Level 8: Requires 116 EXP
  • Level 9: Requires 144 EXP
  • Level 10: Requires 175 EXP
  • Level 11: Requires 209 EXP
  • ...
  • Level 99: Requires 63,449 EXP
  • Level 100: Requires 64,500 EXP
