Referral Earning System

Earn extra rewards by referring users to our platform! Our referral earning system allows you to receive bonuses every time a user you referred deposits funds. Here's how it works:

1. How the Referral System Works

  • Referral Link: When you refer a user to our platform, they will need to sign up using your referral link.
  • 2% Referral Bonus: Once the referred user makes a deposit, 2% of their deposit amount will be automatically credited to your balance as a referral bonus.
  • Automatic Credit: The 2% referral bonus is automatically credited to your balance in the same cryptocurrency the referred user deposited.

3. How Referral Earnings are Credited

Your referral bonus is automatically credited to your account in real-time when a referred user makes a deposit. For example:

  • If a referred user deposits 1 BTC, you will receive 0.02 BTC as a referral bonus.
  • If a referred user deposits 100 USDT, you will receive 2 USDT as a referral bonus.

4. Withdraw Your Referral Earnings

  • Withdraw Anytime: The referral earnings are credited to your balance and can be withdrawn just like any other funds.
  • Instant Withdrawals: Your referral earnings can be withdrawn instantly to your FaucetPay account using the registered email address.
  • No Minimum: There is no minimum withdrawal requirement for referral earnings, allowing you to withdraw even the smallest amounts.

5. How to Get Started

To start earning referral bonuses:

  • Generate Your Referral Link: Visit the Referral Section in your dashboard to get your referral link.
  • Share Your Link: Share your referral link with friends, family, or online communities.
  • Earn Rewards: As soon as they sign up and deposit funds, you’ll earn 2% of their deposits instantly.

Here’s an example of how the referral system works:
